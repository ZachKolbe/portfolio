About Me

Zachary is a student at Penn State pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Human-Centered Design and Development.


Team Member | Foundations of Human-Centered Design and Development

In my role as a team member, I actively contributed to the creation of impactful solutions. We developed a low-fidelity prototype application designed to showcase real-time wait times at on-campus eateries. Additionally, I played a key role in the collaborative development of an application focused on supporting users dealing with mental health challenges. Leveraging Figma, I crafted intuitive wireframes to enhance the UI/UX design.


Golf Pro Shop Assistant

As a Golf Pro Shop Assistant, I excelled in performing diverse responsibilities that ensured the smooth operation of the shop. From handling opening and closing duties, maintaining store cleanliness, and setting up displays to restocking shelves, I demonstrated a commitment to excellence. Working closely with the Golf Pro Shop Manager, I efficiently completed assigned tasks and maintained meticulous records of merchandise sales using the POS system.
